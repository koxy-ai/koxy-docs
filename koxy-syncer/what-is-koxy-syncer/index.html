What is Koxy Syncer

Koxy Syncer is the heart of Koxy AI.

It is a simple command-line tool in the Koxy CLI package that's responsible of keeping your data saved and synced across devices, Koxy AI platform and the edge-cloud.

Understanding how data works in Koxy AI​

Koxy AI is built on top of the decentralized open-source graph database GUN. Understanding how GUN works would be a good practice, but for now let's understand the following concepts:

  • Your data is stored in your browser's local storage. When you use the Koxy AI platform, your data is saved in your browser's local storage. This means that your data exists only in the device that you are currently using.

  • When you open the Koxy AI platform, your browser is connected to our peers. This means that anyone who requests your data from our peers will be able to get it (If they have access to it). However, our peers will only keep a cache of your data for a few minutes, and then they will delete it. This means that it is not a reliable way to host your data and as long as you close your browser the data won't be accessible from anywhere.

  • In order for your data to exist and be accessible from any device, including your local machine or any of Koxy AI products, one of two options must be true:

    1. You have the Koxy AI platform open in your browser. This means that the data will be stored in your browser's local storage and will be accessible as described before.

    2. You have Koxy Syncer running somewhere. This means that Koxy Syncer will keep your data synced across all of your devices, the Koxy AI platform, and the edge-cloud.

What does the Koxy Syncer do​

The Koxy Syncer's main mission is to keep your data live synced across our distributed network of peers, It will only keep the latest updated version of your data. and the data will be accessible from anywhere as long as the syncer is running.

The directory you run the Koxy Syncer in will turn into a Koxy Directory.

Koxy Syncer is user-scoped​

The Koxy Syncer will sync and save all your account's data including all your cloudspaces and It can't be used to sync the data of a specific Cloudspace.

Why my data needs to be synced​

The main idea is that the data should be accessible by our edge-cloud network so when you send a request to your API it can find and compile your API's deployment. and as the edge-cloud is delivered from 35 locations around the world the data should be accessible from anywhere, any time, and at low latency.

Why We don't host your data​

Koxy AI is built on top of the decentralization idea, so It will be against our platform's logics and ideas to host your data on our servers.

Soon Developers will be able to run their Koxy Syncers using our cloud services, but for now You will need to host your data yourself.
