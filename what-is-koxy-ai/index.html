What is Koxy AI

Intro​

Koxy AI is a no-code platform to build AI-powered sevrerless backends with no code and zero config.

We take modern API development to a new era by building a globally distributed system that can serve your API from 35 regions around the world at the edge without writing a single line of code and never minding of configuring or maintaining any physical servers.

Key features​

  • No code: You can build your backend without writing any code.
  • Zero configuration: You don't need to configure any servers or databases.
  • Globally distributed: Your backend is served from 35 regions around the world, so your users get the best possible performance.
  • Scalable: Your backend can scale to meet the needs of your application.
  • Reliable: Your backend is always up and running anytime you send a request to it, even if one region goes down.

Benefits​

  • Save time and money: You don't need to hire a developer to build your backend and we always try our best to offer the best affordable prices.
  • Focus on your core business: You can focus on building your product, not managing servers.
  • Get the best performance: Your backend is served from the closest region to the device that made the request, so your users get the best possible performance.
  • Scale your application easily: Your backend can scale to meet the needs of your application, so you don't have to worry about running out of resources.

Pricing​

Koxy AI offers a variety of pricing plans to fit your needs. You can start with any plan you want and scale your resources as you grow.

We recommend you check our pricing plans to learn more.
