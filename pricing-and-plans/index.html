Pricing & Plans

You can upgrade your Cloudspace at anytime You want. a paid plan is required to run your API.

Available plans​

  • Pro plan.

  • Business plan.

  • Cloud-runs (pay for what you want).

About the Pro plan​

Each Cloudspace in your account is billed separately. When you upgrade your Cloudspace to the Pro plan you get access to all the paid features with 100K cloud-runs included.

Features​

  • Serve your APIs from 35 regions around the world, from the closest region to your users

  • 100K runs included monthly ($3/million runs after that)

  • Unlimited database storage

  • Up to 10ms CPU time per request

  • Every data operation is a cloud run

  • 7-days refund available

Price​

Current price: $12/month/cloudspace.

How to upgrade​

Go to the Cloudspace you want to upgrade and click on plan & usage you see in the cloudspace's sidebar and you will be taken to the plan page.

or go to koxy-ai.web.app/cloudspace/CLOUDSPACE_ID/plan.

Cancelling​

You can go to the plan page and click on Manage plan to cancel your plan.

Cancelling your plan won't get your money back, but the plan won't be renewed in the next month.

You won't lose your cloud-runs, but at the start of the next month you will lose access to running your API in the edge-cloud.

Refund​

You can refund your plan if you fill in the following requirements

  • bought your plan less than 7 days ago.

  • Used less than 1 thousand cloud-runs.

Contact us on this email if you want to refund your plan: multineon@usa.com

About the Bussines plan​

Buy more cloud-runs​

After upgrading to the Pro plan you will be able to buy more cloud runs as you want.

Price​

$3 / 1 million cloud runs.

How to buy more runs​

Go the tha plan page in your Cloudspace and click on Buy more runs. choose the number of runs you want (in millions) and click Buy now.

Limit​

There is currently no limit on how many runs you can buy.

Minimum​

Currently the minimum amount of runs you can buy is 1 million.

Cancelling​

You can't cancel the purchase of a cloud run. It's a one time payment

Refund​

Not supported.

Payments provider​

We are working with Gumroad as our official payments provider.

Each time you make a cloud-run our system needs to check if you have a valid Pro plan (license_key) in a record of time, and Gumroad provides us with a fast reliable API to do this easily.

Most of Gumroad's systems are open-source and you can find them here.
