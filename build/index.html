Welcome

What is Koxy AI​

Koxy AI is a no-code platform to build AI-powered sevrerless backends with no code and zero config.

We make it super easy for you to build your cloud-based backend. served from 35 regions around the world at the edge from the closest region to your users.

Koxy AI gives you a cloud-based serverless backend, Koxy Database (JSON-based) with unlimited storage, drag-and-drop API builder, and more than 80K AI models to power your API.

Our Goal​

Our goal is to make it easy and affordable for developers to build AI-powered secure serverless backends. We believe that APIs are the foundation of modern software development, and we want to make it possible for developers to build the best APIs possible, regardless of their budget or experience using a no-code builder.
